Customer Email Notifications

Open24 sends service emails for bookings, enrollments, account security, and billing continuity. This page summarizes common customer emails, typical send timing, and expected actions.

Email Schedule and Purpose

Email When It Is Sent What To Expect / Action
Booking ConfirmationImmediately after a court booking is created.Confirms date/time/location and payment summary.
Booking UpdatedImmediately after a booking is rescheduled or changed.Shows updated booking details.
Booking CancelledImmediately after cancellation is completed.Confirms cancellation status and related refund/credit outcome if applicable.
Upcoming Booking ReminderUsually about 24 hours before start time.Reminder of upcoming session and location.
Door Access InstructionsAfter qualifying booking or enrollment is confirmed.Provides secure door-access flow and timing window.
Guest Waiver RequestWhen a guest waiver is required and still unsigned.Guest receives signing link before access is allowed.
Course Enrollment ConfirmedImmediately after successful enrollment.Confirms course/activity registration details.
Course Enrollment UpdatedImmediately after enrollment details are changed.Shows updated course session or enrollment information.
Course Enrollment CancelledImmediately after course cancellation/withdrawal.Confirms cancellation and financial outcome where applicable.
Upcoming Course Session ReminderUsually about 24 hours before session start.Reminder for upcoming class/session.
Membership PurchasedImmediately after membership purchase is completed.Confirms plan, billing cadence, and membership start.
Membership RenewedImmediately after successful renewal charge.Confirms continued active membership.
Membership CancelledImmediately when membership cancellation is confirmed.Confirms cancellation timing and end-of-access date (if applicable).
Membership Renewal FailedImmediately when recurring charge fails.Requests payment method update to avoid interruption.
Payment Method Expiring SoonScheduled checks when saved card is close to expiry (typically within 30 days).Advises customer to update card before renewal.
Payment Method ExpiredScheduled checks after card expiry.Requests immediate payment method update.
Payment Method MissingWhen membership is active but no valid payment method is available (for example, post-migration cases).Requests card setup for next billing cycle.
Pass PurchasedImmediately after pass purchase.Confirms pass added to account.
Pass Balance LowScheduled checks when remaining units fall below threshold.Low-balance reminder before pass is exhausted.
Gift Card Purchase ConfirmationImmediately after gift card checkout.Confirms gift card purchase details.
Gift Card DeliveryImmediately when gift card is issued to recipient.Includes gift card code and redemption details.
Gift Card RedeemedImmediately after successful redemption.Confirms account balance update.
Verification CodeWhen account verification is requested.One-time code for secure verification.
Password ResetWhen password reset is requested.Secure reset link with limited validity.
Registration InvitationWhen an invited/migrated account is asked to complete onboarding.Secure link to finish registration and set password.
Promotion Email (Optional)Only when a customer is selected for a campaign.Special offers or announcements; may include opt-out options where applicable.

Timing can vary slightly based on system checks, timezone, and provider delivery latency. Open24 emails never include plaintext passwords and only show masked payment details when needed.
